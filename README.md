## What is LightTable?

LightTable is a decentralized community cookbook built on Lightchain AI. Recipes live on-chain, the people who write the best ones get paid by the people who cook them, and an on-chain AI chef is built into every page to help you actually get dinner on the table.

It is a cookbook that behaves like a tool. Browse and cook from a growing library of community recipes, hand the hard parts to an AI that genuinely understands food, and reward the cooks whose recipes earn their place at your table — all without leaving the app, and all settled in LCAI.

---

## Features

### Recipes that earn their keep
Every recipe is posted on-chain and credited to its creator's wallet. Love a dish? Tip the cook directly in LCAI. Good food gets paid, and the cooks who consistently deliver rise to the top.

### Ask the Kitchen — a real on-chain AI chef
Not a chatbot bolted onto a website. Ask the Kitchen runs as genuine inference on Lightchain's network, with a chef's working knowledge of technique, ratios, and ingredient science:

- **Adapt any recipe** — "I don't have buttermilk," "make it dairy-free," "halve it." It returns a real substitution with the right amount and the reasoning behind it, never just a deletion.
- **Recipe Coach** — sharpen a dish before you publish it.
- **Meal Planner** — turn the cookbook into a week of meals.
- **Pairings & Nutrition** — drinks, sides, and a rough macro read for any recipe.

### Cook Mode
When it is time to actually cook, the screen becomes a clean, hands-free, step-by-step guide that scales every ingredient to your serving count — so the amounts are always right, even with flour on your hands.

### Chef ranks
Share recipes, get cooked, and climb a 30-tier progression from first-timer to master. Recognition that reflects real contribution to the community.

## How it works

LightTable is a fully on-chain dApp. Recipes, tips, upvotes, and credit are settled on Lightchain Mainnet through a verified smart contract, so authorship and rewards are transparent and tamper-resistant.

The AI features are powered by **native Lightchain inference** — the intelligence is the chain doing real, verifiable work, not a wrapper around a third-party API. That distinction is the whole point: an AI cooking assistant whose answers come from the same decentralized network the rest of the app runs on.

Connect any EVM wallet (MetaMask and WalletConnect supported, with a streamlined mobile flow) to post, tip, and use the kitchen.
